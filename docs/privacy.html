Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-22

Introduction

PacePlan is designed as an on-device planning tool. This Privacy Policy explains what data is and is not collected when you use PacePlan and related static website content.

Data Collection

PacePlan does not collect personal data. We do not use analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or cookies.

Data Storage

Any planning data you create in the app remains stored locally on your device. PacePlan does not transmit your training data to PacePlan-operated servers.

Third Parties

PacePlan does not integrate third-party analytics, ad networks, or external tracking services.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product or legal changes. Updated versions will be posted with a revised effective date.
